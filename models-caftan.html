About us

We are ROYAL JAWHARA, a proud purveyor of authentic Moroccan fashion, specializing in exquisitely crafted caftans, takshita, jabador, and jellaba. With deep roots in Moroccan culture and a passion for traditional craftsmanship, we bring the timeless elegance and intricate designs of Moroccan attire to a global audience. Each piece in our collection is meticulously handcrafted by skilled artisans, preserving centuries-old techniques and celebrating the rich heritage of Moroccan fashion. At ROYAL JAWHARA, we strive to showcase the beauty and versatility of Moroccan attire, offering unique and luxurious pieces that reflect the essence of Moroccan craftsmanship and style.
